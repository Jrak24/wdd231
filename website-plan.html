Project Site Plan

Site Name

EcoTravel Adventures

This site focuses on sustainable and eco-friendly travel, providing information on destinations, tips, and booking options.

Site Purpose

The website aims to educate and inspire travelers about sustainable travel options. It will include destination guides, eco-friendly travel tips, and a booking section for sustainable accommodations.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find eco-friendly travel destinations?
  • How can I reduce my carbon footprint while traveling?

Color Schema

  • Forest Green (#228B22): Used for headers and accents.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC): Used for background elements.

Typography

  • Montserrat: Used for headings.
  • Open Sans: Used for body text.
